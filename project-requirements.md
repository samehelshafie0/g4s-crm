G4S KSA Sales & Quotes Platform - Complete System Documentation
Overview
The G4S KSA Sales & Quotes Platform is a comprehensive enterprise resource planning (ERP) solution specifically designed for G4S Security Services in Saudi Arabia. This integrated system manages the entire sales lifecycle from lead generation through quotation, approval, and fulfillment, while also handling warehouse operations, team task management, and comprehensive business intelligence reporting.
